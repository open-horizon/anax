Contract Status

Account:

Name Status

Possible Status Values:
  • Resgistering — Registering the contract on the blockchain.
  • Advertising — Waiting for the agreement proposal from the AgreementBot.
  • Negotiating — AgreementBot has proposed the agreement.
  • Downloading — Proposal accepted and workload started downloading.
  • Running — Workload is running on the device.
  • In Contract — Agreement reached and agreement should be paid out in tokens.
  • In Contract (#num tokens paid) — Agreement reached and tokens paid.
